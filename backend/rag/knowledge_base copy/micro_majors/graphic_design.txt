Major: Graphic Design

Description:
Graphic Design focuses on creating visual communication for print, digital media, branding, advertising, and user experience. It combines creativity, design principles, typography, color, digital tools, and communication skills to produce visual content that informs, attracts, and engages audiences.

Key Topics:
Typography, Color Theory, Digital Design, Visual Communication, Branding, Layout Design, Illustration, User Interface Design, Advertising Design, Digital Media

Required Skills:
Creativity, visual thinking, communication, attention to detail, design sense, problem-solving, time management, teamwork

Recommended High School Subjects:
Art, English, Computer, Mathematics

Related Majors:
Visual Communication Design, Digital Arts and Design, Multimedia, Animation, Advertising, UI/UX Design

Suggested Projects:
Logo design, brand identity project, poster design, social media campaign visuals, mobile app interface mockup, brochure design, packaging design

Possible Careers:
Graphic Designer, Visual Designer, Brand Designer, UI Designer, Illustrator, Creative Designer, Multimedia Designer, Advertising Designer

Universities in Cambodia:
Phnom Penh University of the Arts (PPUA), Paragon International University, University of Cambodia (UC), IT Academy STEP Cambodia Institute, Setec Institute

Beginner Roadmap:
Learn basic design principles -> Practice drawing and visual composition -> Study typography and color theory -> Learn design software such as Photoshop and Illustrator -> Build small branding and poster projects -> Practice digital and interface design -> Create a portfolio -> Gain real-world project experience
