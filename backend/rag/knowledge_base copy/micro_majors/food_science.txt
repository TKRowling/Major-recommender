Major: Food Science

Description:
Food Science focuses on studying food composition, food safety, food processing, quality control, nutrition, and preservation. It combines chemistry, biology, technology, and practical laboratory work to improve food quality, safety, and production for consumers and industry.

Key Topics:
Food Chemistry, Food Microbiology, Food Processing, Food Safety, Quality Control, Nutrition, Food Preservation, Food Analysis, Packaging, Agro-Industry

Required Skills:
Analytical thinking, attention to detail, laboratory skills, problem-solving, scientific observation, communication, teamwork, responsibility

Recommended High School Subjects:
Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, English, Physics

Related Majors:
Food Engineering, Biotechnology, Chemical Engineering, Nutrition, Agriculture, Environmental Science

Suggested Projects:
Food quality testing project, shelf-life analysis, food safety awareness project, nutrition label analysis, small-scale food processing experiment, packaging improvement study

Possible Careers:
Food Scientist, Quality Control Officer, Food Safety Officer, Laboratory Analyst, Production Supervisor, Research Assistant, Food Processing Specialist

Universities in Cambodia:
Institute of Technology of Cambodia (ITC), Royal University of Agriculture (RUA), Royal University of Phnom Penh (RUPP)

Beginner Roadmap:
Build strong chemistry and biology foundation -> Learn basic food science concepts -> Study food safety and microbiology -> Practice laboratory testing and analysis -> Learn food processing and preservation methods -> Understand quality control and packaging -> Work on small food-related projects -> Gain practical experience through research or internships
