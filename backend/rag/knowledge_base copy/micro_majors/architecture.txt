Major: Architecture

Description:
Architecture focuses on designing buildings, spaces, and the built environment to meet human needs in functional, safe, sustainable, and visually appealing ways. It combines creativity, technical knowledge, spatial thinking, and design methods to shape homes, workplaces, public spaces, and cities.

Key Topics:
Architectural Design, Design Studio, Architectural Drawing, Building Structures, Construction Materials, Sustainable Design, Urban Space, Spatial Planning, History of Architecture, Computer-Aided Design

Required Skills:
Creativity, visual thinking, problem-solving, spatial awareness, communication, attention to detail, technical drawing ability, teamwork

Recommended High School Subjects:
Mathematics, Physics, English, Art

Related Majors:
Civil Engineering, Interior Design, Urban Planning, Construction Management, Graphic Design

Suggested Projects:
House design concept, community space design, sustainable building concept, 3D building model, interior and exterior layout plan, public space redesign

Possible Careers:
Architect, Architectural Designer, Urban Designer, Interior Designer, Draftsperson, Design Consultant, Project Coordinator

Universities in Cambodia:
Paragon International University, Pannasastra University of Cambodia (PUC), Cambodia University of Technology and Science (CamTech), Royal University of Fine Arts(RUFA), Norton University, Limkokwing University of Creative Technology, Institute of Technology of Cambodia (ITC), Cambodian Mekong University, Asia Euro University

Beginner Roadmap:
Learn basic drawing and visual composition -> Study design principles and spatial thinking -> Practice architectural sketching and technical drawing -> Learn about structures and construction materials -> Use design software such as AutoCAD or SketchUp -> Explore sustainable and urban design concepts -> Build a design portfolio -> Gain practical studio or project experience
