Major: International Economics

Description:
International Economics focuses on how countries trade, invest, and interact in the global economy. It combines economic theory, trade analysis, finance, statistics, and policy understanding to study international markets, economic development, and the movement of goods, services, and capital across borders.

Key Topics:
International Trade, International Finance, Microeconomics, Macroeconomics, Mathematical Economics, Applied Statistics, Economic Development, Trade Policy, Global Markets, Economic Analysis

Required Skills:
Analytical thinking, problem-solving, communication, critical thinking, quantitative ability, data interpretation, research ability, attention to detail

Recommended High School Subjects:
Mathematics, English, Economics, Geography

Related Majors:
Economics, International Business, Finance, International Relations, Business Administration

Suggested Projects:
Trade flow analysis, export-import case study, exchange rate analysis, market research report, economic policy review, regional trade comparison

Possible Careers:
Economist, Trade Analyst, Market Research Analyst, Policy Research Assistant, Economic Consultant, International Business Officer, Development Project Officer

Universities in Cambodia:
Royal University of Phnom Penh (RUPP), Paragon International University, The National University of Management (MUN), Pannasastra University of Cambodia (PUC)

Beginner Roadmap:
Build strong mathematics and economics foundation -> Learn microeconomics and macroeconomics -> Study international trade and finance -> Practice statistics and data analysis -> Explore global market and policy issues -> Work on economic case studies and reports -> Build research and communication skills -> Gain internship or project experience
