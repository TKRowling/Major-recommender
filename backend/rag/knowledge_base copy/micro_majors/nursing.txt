Major: Nursing

Description:
Nursing focuses on caring for individuals, families, and communities to maintain and improve health. It combines medical knowledge, patient care, communication, ethics, and practical clinical skills to support treatment, recovery, prevention, and health education in hospitals and community settings.

Key Topics:
Fundamentals of Nursing, Anatomy and Physiology, Patient Care, Community Health, Maternal and Child Health, Medical-Surgical Nursing, Pharmacology, Infection Prevention, Ethics in Nursing, Clinical Practice

Required Skills:
Communication, empathy, responsibility, attention to detail, teamwork, critical thinking, problem-solving, patience, time management

Recommended High School Subjects:
Biology, Chemistry, English, Mathematics

Related Majors:
Midwifery, Public Health, Medicine, Medical Laboratory Technology, Pharmacy

Suggested Projects:
Health education campaign, patient care case study, community health survey, infection prevention awareness project, maternal health support project, nutrition education activity

Possible Careers:
Registered Nurse, Clinical Nurse, Community Health Nurse, Hospital Nurse, Nurse Educator, Public Health Nurse, Ward Supervisor

Universities in Cambodia:
University of Puthisastra (UP), University of Health Sciences (UHS), Life University

Beginner Roadmap:
Study biology and basic health science -> Learn human anatomy and physiology -> Build communication and patient-care skills -> Understand hygiene and infection prevention -> Study nursing fundamentals -> Practice clinical observation and case discussion -> Join health-related volunteering or training -> Continue developing professional ethics and practical skills
