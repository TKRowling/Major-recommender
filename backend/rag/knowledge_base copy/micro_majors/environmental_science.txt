Major: Environmental Science

Description:
Environmental Science focuses on understanding the environment and solving problems related to pollution, natural resources, sustainability, and human impact on ecosystems. It combines science, research, and practical decision-making to help protect the environment and support sustainable development.

Key Topics:
Environmental Science, Environmental Chemistry, Ecology, Natural Resource Management, Sustainable Development, Environmental Economics, Statistics, Environmental Pollution, Water Resources, Climate and Environmental Issues

Required Skills:
Analytical thinking, problem-solving, observation, research ability, critical thinking, communication, data interpretation, attention to detail, teamwork

Recommended High School Subjects:
Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, English, Geography

Related Majors:
Environmental Engineering, Biology, Chemistry, Natural Resource Management, Public Health, Agriculture

Suggested Projects:
Water quality testing project, waste management study, community recycling awareness project, environmental impact case study, air pollution observation project, biodiversity survey

Possible Careers:
Environmental Officer, Environmental Scientist, Sustainability Officer, Natural Resource Manager, Environmental Research Assistant, NGO Project Officer, Environmental Consultant

Universities in Cambodia:
Royal University of Phnom Penh (RUPP), Pannasastra University of Cambodia (PUC), Institute of Technology of Cambodia (ITC), Royal University of Agriculture (RUA)

Beginner Roadmap:
Build strong biology and chemistry foundation -> Learn basic environmental concepts -> Study ecosystems, pollution, and natural resources -> Practice data collection and analysis -> Learn sustainability and environmental policy basics -> Join small research or field projects -> Build reports and case studies -> Gain practical experience through internships or community projects
