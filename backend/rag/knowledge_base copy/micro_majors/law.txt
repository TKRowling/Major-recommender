Major: Law

Description:
Law focuses on the study of legal systems, rules, and principles that govern society. It combines critical thinking, reasoning, and communication to understand rights, responsibilities, justice, and the application of laws in different areas such as business, government, and international relations.

Key Topics:
Constitutional Law, Civil Law, Criminal Law, Contract Law, International Law, Administrative Law, Human Rights Law, Legal Research, Legal Writing

Required Skills:
Critical thinking, analytical reasoning, communication, argumentation, problem-solving, attention to detail, research ability, ethics

Recommended High School Subjects:
English, History, Civics, Geography, Mathematics

Related Majors:
Political Science, International Relations, Public Administration, Criminology, Business Law

Suggested Projects:
Legal case study analysis, mock trial simulation, human rights research project, legal awareness campaign, policy analysis report, contract drafting practice

Possible Careers:
Lawyer, Legal Advisor, Judge, Prosecutor, Legal Officer, Policy Analyst, Government Officer, NGO Legal Specialist

Universities in Cambodia:
Royal University of Law and Economics (RULE), University of Cambodia (UC), Pannasastra University of Cambodia (PUC), American University of Phnom Penh (AUPP), National University of Management (NUM), Norton University, Build Bright University (BBU), Asia Europe University (AEU), Phnom Penh International University (PPIU)

Beginner Roadmap:
Build strong reading and writing skills -> Learn basic legal concepts and systems -> Study constitutional and civil law -> Practice legal analysis and case studies -> Develop argument and communication skills -> Learn legal research methods -> Participate in mock trials or debates -> Gain internship or legal experience
