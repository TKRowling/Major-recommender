Major: Management of Information Systems

Description:
Management of Information Systems focuses on using technology, data, and information systems to support business operations and decision-making. It combines business knowledge, computer-based systems, and management skills to help organizations design, analyze, implement, and manage information systems effectively.

Key Topics:
Information Systems, Database Management, Business Processes, Systems Analysis and Design, IT Project Management, Business Intelligence, Enterprise Systems, Systems Integration, Data Management, Digital Transformation

Required Skills:
Problem-solving, analytical thinking, communication, teamwork, business understanding, technical understanding, attention to detail, adaptability

Recommended High School Subjects:
Mathematics, English, Computer, Economics, Business Studies

Related Majors:
Information Technology, Computer Science, Business Administration, Data Science, Accounting, Digital Business

Suggested Projects:
Inventory management system, student information system, sales reporting dashboard, business process automation project, customer database system, IT project planning case study

Possible Careers:
MIS Officer, Business Analyst, Systems Analyst, IT Project Coordinator, Database Administrator, ERP Support Specialist, Business Intelligence Assistant

Universities in Cambodia:
Paragon International University

Beginner Roadmap:
Learn computer fundamentals -> Study basic business concepts -> Learn spreadsheets and databases -> Understand information systems and business processes -> Practice systems analysis and reporting -> Learn project management and enterprise systems -> Build simple business-tech projects -> Gain internship or real-world experience
