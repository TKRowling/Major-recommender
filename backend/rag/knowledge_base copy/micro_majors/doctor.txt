Major: Medicine

Description:
Medicine focuses on the study of human health, disease prevention, diagnosis, treatment, and patient care. It combines medical science, clinical knowledge, ethics, communication, and practical hospital training to prepare students to care for patients and improve public health.

Key Topics:
Human Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Pathology, Pharmacology, Microbiology, Internal Medicine, Surgery, Pediatrics, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Community Medicine, Clinical Practice

Required Skills:
Communication, empathy, critical thinking, problem-solving, responsibility, attention to detail, teamwork, patience, ethical awareness

Recommended High School Subjects:
Biology, Chemistry, English, Mathematics, Physics

Related Majors:
Nursing, Pharmacy, Dentistry, Public Health, Medical Laboratory Technology

Suggested Projects:
Community health awareness project, disease prevention campaign, patient case study, nutrition education activity, public health survey, clinical observation report

Possible Careers:
Medical Doctor, General Practitioner, Hospital Doctor, Community Health Doctor, Medical Officer, Clinical Researcher, Public Health Physician

Universities in Cambodia:
University of Health Sciences (UHS), University of Puthisastra (UP), Health Science Institute of R.C.A.F, 

Beginner Roadmap:
Build strong biology and chemistry foundation -> Study human anatomy and physiology -> Develop communication and patient-care skills -> Learn basic disease prevention and health science concepts -> Strengthen ethics and responsibility -> Gain clinical exposure and observation -> Practice case-based learning -> Continue medical training and professional development
