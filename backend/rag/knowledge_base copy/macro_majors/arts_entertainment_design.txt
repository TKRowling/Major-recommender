Macro-Major: Arts, Entertainment, and Design

Description:
Arts, Entertainment, and Design focuses on creative expression, visual communication, media production, performance, and design innovation. It combines creativity, technical skills, communication, and cultural understanding to produce artistic works, entertainment content, and design solutions for different audiences and industries.

Common Micro-Majors:
Graphic Design, Interior Design, Architecture, Animation, Fashion Design, Fine Arts, Visual Communication, Multimedia Design, Music, Film and Media, Performing Arts

Key Topics:
Design Principles, Visual Communication, Drawing, Color Theory, Typography, Media Production, Creative Process, Branding, Performance, Digital Design, User Experience, Art History

Common Skills:
Creativity, communication, visual thinking, attention to detail, problem-solving, collaboration, adaptability, presentation skills, time management

Recommended High School Subjects:
Art, English, Computer, Mathematics, History

Common Careers:
Graphic Designer, Interior Designer, Animator, Multimedia Designer, Fashion Designer, Artist, Music Producer, Performer, Creative Director, Content Creator

Learning Style:
Students in this field often work on studio projects, creative assignments, portfolios, performances, and presentations. The learning process emphasizes imagination, experimentation, practical production, and the development of personal style and technical ability.

Related Macro-Majors:
Marketing and Sales, Construction, Digital Technology, Education

Example Career Paths:
- Graphic Design → Junior Designer → Graphic Designer → Creative Director
- Multimedia → Content Creator → Multimedia Designer → Art Director
- Performing Arts → Performer → Production Coordinator → Creative Producer
- Interior Design → Design Assistant → Interior Designer → Design Consultant
