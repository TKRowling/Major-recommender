Macro-Major: Agriculture

Description:
Agriculture focuses on the production of crops, livestock, and food resources, as well as the sustainable management of land and natural resources. It combines science, technology, and environmental knowledge to improve food security, farming efficiency, and agricultural development.

Common Micro-Majors:
Agronomy, Animal Science, Food Science, Agricultural Engineering, Horticulture, Aquaculture, Plant Science, Agricultural Economics, Soil Science, Crop Science

Key Topics:
Crop Production, Animal Production, Soil Science, Irrigation Systems, Agricultural Technology, Pest Management, Food Production, Agribusiness, Sustainable Agriculture, Agricultural Economics

Common Skills:
Observation, problem-solving, analytical thinking, environmental awareness, practical skills, teamwork, adaptability, data interpretation

Recommended High School Subjects:
Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, Geography, English

Common Careers:
Agricultural Officer, Farm Manager, Agronomist, Animal Scientist, Food Production Specialist, Agricultural Technician, Agribusiness Manager, Research Assistant

Learning Style:
Students in this field often work on field activities, laboratory experiments, agricultural projects, and practical training. The learning process combines scientific knowledge with hands-on experience in farming, production, and resource management.

Related Macro-Majors:
Environmental and Natural Resources, Food Science and Nutrition, Engineering

Example Career Paths:
- Crop Production → Field Technician → Agronomist
- Animal Science → Livestock Assistant → Animal Production Specialist
- Agribusiness → Business Officer → Agribusiness Manager
- Agricultural Technology → Technician → Agricultural Engineer
