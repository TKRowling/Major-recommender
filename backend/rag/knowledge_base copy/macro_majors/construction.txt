Macro-Major: Construction

Description:
Construction focuses on planning, designing, building, and managing structures such as houses, buildings, roads, and infrastructure projects. It combines technical knowledge, design understanding, project management, and practical problem-solving to create safe, functional, and sustainable built environments.

Common Micro-Majors:
Civil Engineering, Architecture, Construction Management, Quantity Surveying, Interior Design, Urban Planning, Building Technology, Structural Engineering

Key Topics:
Construction Materials, Building Design, Structural Systems, Project Management, Site Management, Cost Estimation, Technical Drawing, Surveying, Safety Management, Infrastructure Development

Common Skills:
Problem-solving, technical thinking, spatial awareness, teamwork, communication, attention to detail, planning, leadership, mathematics ability

Recommended High School Subjects:
Mathematics, Physics, English, Art, Computer

Common Careers:
Civil Engineer, Architect, Construction Manager, Site Engineer, Quantity Surveyor, Project Coordinator, Building Inspector, Interior Designer

Learning Style:
Students in this field often work on technical drawings, design projects, calculations, construction case studies, and practical site-related tasks. The learning process combines theory, design, planning, and real-world application in building and infrastructure projects.

Related Macro-Majors:
Engineering, Arts, Entertainment, and Design, Energy and Natural Resources, Advanced Manufacturing

Example Career Paths:
- Civil Engineering → Junior Site Engineer → Site Engineer → Project Manager
- Architecture → Design Assistant → Architect → Senior Architect
- Construction Management → Site Coordinator → Construction Manager → Operations Director
- Quantity Surveying → Estimation Assistant → Quantity Surveyor → Cost Manager
