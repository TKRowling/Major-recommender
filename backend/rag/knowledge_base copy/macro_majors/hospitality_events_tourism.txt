Macro-Major: Hospitality, Events, and Tourism

Description:
Hospitality, Events, and Tourism focuses on providing services related to travel, accommodation, food, and event management. It combines customer service, communication, management, and cultural understanding to create positive experiences for guests, tourists, and event participants.

Common Micro-Majors:
Tourism Management, Hospitality Management, Hotel Management, Event Management, Travel and Tourism, Culinary Arts, Resort Management, Airline Management

Key Topics:
Tourism Management, Hospitality Operations, Event Planning, Customer Service, Food and Beverage Management, Travel Industry, Cultural Tourism, Marketing for Tourism, Service Management

Common Skills:
Communication, customer service, teamwork, problem-solving, adaptability, organization, interpersonal skills, cultural awareness

Recommended High School Subjects:
English, Geography, Business Studies, Mathematics

Common Careers:
Hotel Manager, Event Planner, Tour Guide, Travel Consultant, Hospitality Manager, Airline Staff, Restaurant Manager, Customer Service Officer

Learning Style:
Students in this field often engage in practical training, internships, role-playing, service simulations, and real-world projects. The learning process emphasizes communication, service quality, and hands-on experience in hospitality and tourism environments.

Related Macro-Majors:
Management and Entrepreneurship, Marketing and Sales, Arts and Communication

Example Career Paths:
- Hospitality → Hotel Staff → Hotel Supervisor → Hotel Manager
- Tourism → Tour Guide → Travel Consultant → Tourism Manager
- Events → Event Assistant → Event Coordinator → Event Manager
- Culinary → Kitchen Assistant → Chef → Executive Chef
