Macro-Major: Marketing and Sales

Description:
Marketing and Sales focuses on understanding customer needs, promoting products or services, building brand awareness, and generating revenue. It combines creativity, communication, strategy, and data analysis to attract customers, increase sales, and grow businesses in competitive markets.

Common Micro-Majors:
Marketing, Digital Marketing, Sales Management, Advertising, Branding, Public Relations, E-commerce, Content Marketing, International Marketing

Key Topics:
Marketing Principles, Consumer Behavior, Digital Marketing, Branding, Advertising, Sales Strategy, Market Research, Social Media Marketing, Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Business Communication

Common Skills:
Communication, creativity, persuasion, presentation skills, analytical thinking, problem-solving, adaptability, teamwork, customer understanding

Recommended High School Subjects:
English, Mathematics, Economics, Business Studies

Common Careers:
Marketing Executive, Sales Executive, Digital Marketing Specialist, Brand Manager, Social Media Manager, Sales Manager, Marketing Analyst, Business Development Officer

Learning Style:
Students in this field often work on case studies, marketing campaigns, presentations, group projects, and real-world business scenarios. The focus is on creativity, communication, and applying strategies to attract and retain customers.

Related Macro-Majors:
Management and Entrepreneurship, Economics and Finance, Media and Communication

Example Career Paths:
- Marketing → Marketing Executive → Brand Manager
- Sales → Sales Representative → Sales Manager
- Digital Marketing → Social Media Specialist → Digital Marketing Manager
- Business Development → Business Development Officer → Growth Manager
