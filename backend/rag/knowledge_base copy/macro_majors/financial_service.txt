Macro-Major: Financial Services

Description:
Financial Services focuses on managing money, financial planning, investment, banking operations, insurance, and risk management for individuals, businesses, and organizations. It combines financial knowledge, analytical thinking, decision-making, and business understanding to support economic growth and financial stability.

Common Micro-Majors:
Accounting, Finance, Banking and Finance, Financial Management, Investment and Securities, Insurance, Auditing, FinTech, Economics

Key Topics:
Financial Management, Banking Operations, Investment, Financial Analysis, Risk Management, Insurance, Auditing, Corporate Finance, Financial Markets, FinTech

Common Skills:
Analytical thinking, problem-solving, numerical ability, attention to detail, decision-making, communication, responsibility, critical thinking

Recommended High School Subjects:
Mathematics, English, Economics, Business Studies

Common Careers:
Accountant, Financial Analyst, Banker, Auditor, Investment Analyst, Credit Officer, Insurance Officer, Finance Manager, Risk Analyst

Learning Style:
Students in this field often work on financial case studies, calculations, reports, market analysis, and business scenarios. The learning process emphasizes numerical accuracy, analytical reasoning, and practical understanding of financial systems and institutions.

Related Macro-Majors:
Management and Entrepreneurship, Marketing and Sales, Public Service and Safety

Example Career Paths:
- Accounting → Junior Accountant → Senior Accountant → Finance Manager
- Banking and Finance → Bank Teller → Credit Officer → Branch Manager
- Investment → Financial Analyst → Investment Analyst → Portfolio Manager
- Insurance → Insurance Officer → Risk Analyst → Insurance Manager
