Macro-Major: Supply Chain and Transportation

Description:
Supply Chain and Transportation focuses on the movement of goods, services, materials, and information from producers to consumers. It combines logistics, operations, planning, transportation systems, and business coordination to improve efficiency, reduce cost, and support trade and distribution.

Common Micro-Majors:
Logistics and Supply Chain Management, Transportation Management, Warehouse Management, Operations Management, Procurement, Shipping and Maritime Studies, Aviation Management, Export-Import Management

Key Topics:
Logistics, Supply Chain Management, Transportation Systems, Inventory Management, Procurement, Operations Planning, Distribution, Warehouse Management, Trade Operations, Route Planning

Common Skills:
Organization, problem-solving, analytical thinking, communication, teamwork, planning, time management, adaptability, decision-making

Recommended High School Subjects:
Mathematics, English, Geography, Economics, Business Studies

Common Careers:
Logistics Officer, Supply Chain Analyst, Procurement Officer, Warehouse Supervisor, Transportation Coordinator, Operations Officer, Import-Export Officer, Distribution Manager

Learning Style:
Students in this field often work on case studies, process planning, business operations tasks, and real-world logistics scenarios. The learning process emphasizes coordination, efficiency, decision-making, and practical understanding of transportation and supply systems.

Related Macro-Majors:
Management and Entrepreneurship, Financial Services, Hospitality, Events, and Tourism, Advanced Manufacturing

Example Career Paths:
- Logistics → Logistics Assistant → Logistics Officer → Supply Chain Manager
- Procurement → Purchasing Assistant → Procurement Officer → Procurement Manager
- Transportation → Operations Assistant → Transportation Coordinator → Transport Manager
- Warehouse Operations → Storekeeper → Warehouse Supervisor → Distribution Manager
