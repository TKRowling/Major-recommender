Macro-Major: Education

Description:
Education focuses on teaching, learning, and developing knowledge, skills, and values in individuals and communities. It combines pedagogy, communication, psychology, and subject expertise to support student learning, academic development, and lifelong education.

Common Micro-Majors:
Education, Teaching, Early Childhood Education, Primary Education, Secondary Education, Educational Management, TESOL (Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages), Special Education, Curriculum Development

Key Topics:
Teaching Methods, Learning Theories, Educational Psychology, Curriculum Design, Classroom Management, Assessment and Evaluation, Educational Technology, Child Development, Instructional Design

Common Skills:
Communication, patience, empathy, leadership, organization, creativity, problem-solving, adaptability, teamwork

Recommended High School Subjects:
English, Mathematics, History, Geography, Psychology

Common Careers:
Teacher, Lecturer, Education Officer, School Administrator, Curriculum Developer, Trainer, Education Consultant, Academic Coordinator

Learning Style:
Students in this field often engage in teaching practice, lesson planning, classroom observation, group activities, and presentations. The learning process emphasizes communication, interaction, and practical teaching experience.

Related Macro-Majors:
Social Sciences, Public Service and Safety, Arts and Humanities

Example Career Paths:
- Teaching → Assistant Teacher → Teacher → Senior Teacher
- Education Management → Education Officer → School Administrator
- TESOL → English Instructor → Language Program Coordinator
- Curriculum Development → Instructional Designer → Education Specialist
